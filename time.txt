                                      Time
                                      ====

Conversions
-----------

date -d@      # unix -> std
date -d'' +%s # std  -> unix

Regexes
-------

Date:
#    Year     Month    Day
# 1900-2099 / 01-12 / 01-31
^(?:19|20)\d\d/(?:0?[1-9]|1[0-2])/(?:0?[1-9]|[12]\d|3[01])$

Time:
# 03:24:44 Pm
^(?:0?\d|1\d|2[0-3])(?::[0-5]\d){1,2}[[:space:]]*(?:[aA]|[pP])[mM]$
