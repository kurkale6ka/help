Stop all CPU functions (kill all?), keep powered on
---------------------------------------------------

halt
halt -p
halt --reboot

Power off
---------

poweroff
poweroff --halt
poweroff --reboot

Reboot
------

reboot
reboot -p
reboot --halt

Schedule power down
-------------------

shutdown -P, --poweroff (or -h)
shutdown -H, --halt
shutdown -r, --reboot

examples:

shutdown -h now
shutdown -r +5 'The system will be rebooted in 5 minutes!'
shutdown -r now
shutdown -r +0
